# Agency Page + Free-Audit Magnet

**Purpose:** Spec a service/agency landing page paired with a free-audit lead magnet — the page type designed to convert SEO traffic into qualified leads, not just rankings.

**When to run this:** Building or refreshing a service page for an agency/consultancy offer (directly applicable to Ellocent Labs service pages and to VirtuNova's own positioning — keep the two builds separate per usual).

**Inputs needed:**
- Service description, target client profile, pricing model (if disclosed)
- Proof assets available (case studies, testimonials, sample reports)

**Process:**
1. Lead with the outcome the client cares about, not the methodology — "Get a prioritized fix list in 48 hours," not "We perform comprehensive technical audits."
2. Structure the page around objection-handling in order: what you get → proof it works → how it's different from a generic agency → pricing/process clarity → the free-audit CTA.
3. Design the free-audit magnet as a genuinely useful, narrow deliverable (e.g., a 10-point scored report using `seo-technical-audit.md` logic) — not a sales call disguised as an audit. The audit itself is the trust-builder.
4. Include the E-E-A-T signals this page needs to rank for its own commercial-intent terms: named team bios, real case studies with numbers, transparent process description.
5. Set one primary CTA (claim the free audit) and remove competing CTAs — agency pages that offer five different actions convert worse than ones that offer one.

**Output format:**
Page brief: Hero promise | Objection-handling section order | Free-audit magnet scope (exact deliverable, turnaround time) | Proof assets to feature | Single CTA copy.

**Quality bar:** If the "free audit" magnet can't be delivered in the stated turnaround without manual heroics every time, redesign the scope — a lead magnet that breaks your delivery capacity isn't a lead magnet, it's a liability.
