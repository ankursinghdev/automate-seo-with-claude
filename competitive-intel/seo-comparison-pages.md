# "X vs Y" Comparison Page Builder

**Purpose:** Identify and spec comparison pages ("X vs Y", "best alternatives to X") that capture high-intent decision-stage traffic — this is the file that was incorrectly named `seo-subdomain.md` in the source planning graphic; renamed here because it shares nothing with the actual subdomain/cannibalization audit below.

**When to run this:** Once a niche has 2+ named competitors with search volume against their brand names, or once you have 2+ comparable products/services worth contrasting.

**Inputs needed:**
- Your product/service + named competitors with measurable "[Competitor] vs [You]" or "[Competitor] alternatives" search volume
- Honest feature/pricing/positioning comparison data (do not fabricate competitor specifics)

**Process:**
1. Check search volume for `[brand] vs [brand]`, `[brand] alternative`, and `best [category] for [use case]` patterns — only build pages where real volume exists.
2. Build the comparison on dimensions a buyer actually decides on (price, support, integrations, learning curve) — not vanity features.
3. Take a clear position without misrepresenting the competitor — credibility on this page format is the entire point; getting caught spinning facts kills conversion and invites a takedown request.
4. Structure for both human skimmers and AI Overview extraction: a comparison table near the top, a direct verdict sentence, then supporting detail.
5. Link to relevant proof (case studies, pricing page, free trial) as the page's single CTA.

**Output format:**
Page brief: Comparison angle | Search volume for the pattern | Comparison table columns | Verdict sentence | CTA | Internal links to proof.

**Quality bar:** If you can't list one honest area where the competitor is genuinely better, the page reads as biased and AI Overviews are less likely to cite it as a neutral comparison.
