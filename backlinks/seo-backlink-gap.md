# Domains Linking Rivals, Not You

**Purpose:** Find the specific referring domains that link to competitors but not to you — the highest-conversion-rate outreach list there is, because these sites have already proven they'll link to someone in your exact space.

**When to run this:** Whenever active link-building/outreach capacity opens up. Pairs with `seo-competitor-gap-analysis.md` for content gaps that double as link-attraction assets.

**Inputs needed:**
- Your domain + 2–3 competitor domains
- Backlink data source

**Process:**
1. Pull referring domains for each competitor, then subtract your own referring domain list — the remainder is the gap.
2. Filter out domains that are clearly competitor-specific (their own subsidiaries, sponsored partners, paid directories with no editorial standard) — keep only domains likely to link to *any* credible player in the space.
3. Segment the remaining list by link type: editorial mention, resource page, guest post host, directory, podcast/interview — outreach angle differs by type.
4. Check each domain's linking page for *why* they linked (often: a specific stat, tool, or quote) — that tells you what asset to pitch, not just "please link to us."
5. Prioritize by DR × topical relevance × realistic outreach effort, not DR alone.

**Output format:**
`Referring domain | DR | Link type | What they linked to (on competitor) | Outreach angle | Priority`

**Quality bar:** A gap list with no outreach angle column is just a list of websites — the angle is the actual deliverable.
